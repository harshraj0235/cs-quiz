FAQs - Quiz Maker

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Quiz Maker free?

Yes, Quiz Maker offers a free plan with basic features. Advanced features may require a premium subscription.

Do I need to signup to make my own quiz?

Yes, you need to sign up for an account to create and manage quizzes on Quiz Maker.

How do I see who has completed my quiz?

You can view quiz completion statistics and individual responses in your Quiz Maker dashboard after participants have submitted their quizzes.

How to make online courses?

To create online courses, you can use e-learning platforms that support course creation. Quiz Maker may integrate with certain platforms or offer course creation features directly.

How do I access my results and existing quizzes?

Log in to your Quiz Maker account and navigate to the dashboard. There, you'll find all your quizzes along with detailed results and analytics.

Where do I create my quiz?

You can create quizzes directly on the Quiz Maker website after signing in to your account. Use the quiz creation tools provided in your dashboard.
